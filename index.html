📖 HOW TO PLAY

THE GOAL

It's a card game crossed with a dungeon crawl. Fight your way up the map, win battles with your deck of cards, collect better cards and relics as you go, and defeat the Federal Reserve at the top. If your HP (health) hits 0, the run is over — you start fresh.

YOUR TURN, STEP BY STEP

1. Each turn you get 3 Capital ($) — this is your budget for playing cards.
2. Every card shows its cost in the gold coin on its corner. Drag a card onto an enemy to attack it, or drag it up to the battlefield to play it. Clicking works too.
3. Red cards attack, blue cards protect you, purple cards do tricks (draw more cards, heal, power up).
4. When you're done (or out of Capital), press END TURN. The enemy acts, then you get a fresh hand of 5 cards and full Capital.

READ THE ENEMY'S MIND

The box above each enemy shows exactly what it will do next turn — e.g. "Audit: 8 dmg" means it will hit you for 8. If you have Block 🛡, block absorbs damage first. Block disappears at the start of your next turn, so block when a hit is coming, attack when it isn't.

THE JARGON (hover anything in-game for a reminder)

Block 🛡 — a shield. Absorbs that much damage, then expires next turn.
Vulnerable — takes 50% MORE damage. Great to put on enemies.
Weak — deals 25% LESS damage. Great to put on enemies, bad on you.
Strength — all your attacks hit harder (+1 damage per point).
Debt — you lose that much HP at the end of every turn. Clear it with Tax Shelter — or weaponize it with Junk Bonds.
Retain — your Block survives into next turn instead of expiring.
Exhaust — the card is used ONCE per battle, then gone until the next battle.

BETWEEN BATTLES

⚔️ Combat — a normal fight, rewards a new card.  💀 Elite — harder fight, also drops a relic (permanent bonus).  🏦 Exchange — spend gold on cards.  ☕ Meeting — heal, remove a card, or upgrade one.  ❓ Event — a surprise with choices.

ONE TIP

A small deck of good cards beats a big pile of mediocre ones — you'll draw your best cards more often. Don't grab every card you're offered!
